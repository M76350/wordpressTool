How to Calculate Volumetric Weight for Courier Charges From India To Luxembourg

Before shipping your parcel internationally, it’s important to understand how volumetric weight affects courier charges. Freight companies charge based on the higher value between actual weight and volumetric weight.

Volumetric Weight Formula

Volumetric Weight (kg) = (Length × Width × Height in cm) ÷ 5000

Example Calculation

Suppose you’re sending a 10 kg box with the following measurements:

  • Length: 42 cm
  • Width: 63 cm
  • Height: 42 cm

Then,
Volumetric Weight = (42 × 63 × 42) ÷ 5000 = 22.2 kg

Even though your actual weight is 10 kg, the volumetric weight (22.2 kg) is higher — so your shipment will be billed under the 22–23 kg rate slab.
That’s why correct packaging and measurement are key to getting the best courier rates to Luxembourg from India.
