How to Calculate Volumetric Weight for Courier Charges from Delhi to Denmark

Before shipping internationally, it is important to understand how volumetric weight affects courier pricing. Courier companies usually bill shipments on the higher of actual weight or volumetric weight.

Volumetric Weight Formula

Volumetric Weight (kg) = (Length × Width × Height in cm) ÷ 5000

Example Calculation

Suppose you are sending a 10 kg box with the following dimensions:

  • Length: 42 cm
  • Width: 63 cm
  • Height: 42 cm

Volumetric Weight = (42 × 63 × 42) ÷ 5000 = 22.2 kg

Even though the actual weight is 10 kg, the volumetric weight is higher, so the courier charges will be based on the higher slab. That is why correct measurement and compact packing are important for getting better shipping rates.

📦 Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) – Courier Charges from Delhi to Denmark

1. How much is the cheapest courier from Delhi to Denmark?
Affordable courier options from Delhi to Denmark can start from around ₹499+ per kg depending on parcel weight, dimensions, service type, and delivery timeline.

2. How long does delivery from Delhi to Denmark usually take?
Express courier services generally take around 4–6 business days, while economy services may take approximately 7–12 business days.

3. Which are the common courier companies for shipping from Delhi to Denmark?
Popular international courier partners include DHL, FedEx, UPS, Aramex, and other recognized logistics networks depending on service availability.

4. How are courier charges from Delhi to Denmark calculated?
Courier rates are usually based on the higher of actual weight or volumetric weight. Formula: (Length × Width × Height) ÷ 5000.

5. Can I send food items or homemade goods to Denmark?
Yes, approved non-perishable food items such as snacks, spices, and sweets may be shipped, subject to courier and destination regulations.

6. Can medicines be shipped from Delhi to Denmark?
Yes, approved medicines for personal use can often be sent with a valid prescription and invoice, depending on shipment rules.

7. Are customs charges included in courier pricing?
Usually, customs duties and import taxes are not included in the courier charge unless clearly mentioned. These are commonly payable by the receiver.

8. How can I track my shipment from Delhi to Denmark?
You can track your parcel using the AWB number on the courier partner’s website or on your shipment tracking page.

9. What items are restricted for shipping to Denmark?
Restricted items generally include flammables, firearms, narcotics, hazardous goods, certain liquids, and perishable items. Always confirm before booking.

10. Is doorstep pickup available in Delhi?
Yes, pickup service is available in many locations across Delhi depending on courier partner coverage.
